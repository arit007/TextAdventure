My Text Adventure Instructions

To Win
------------------------
Follow this path
-Bridge
-Corridor
-Cafeteria
-Supply Closet -> pick up the key here

Next travel back to the Corridor.
Go into the Barracks -> into the Escape Pods.



To Lose
------------------------
Follow this path
-Bridge
-Corridor
-Cafeteria
-Supply Closet -> pick up the key here

Next travel back to the Corridor and go into the Hangar.
